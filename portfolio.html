S.E.R.E Training

Whether it’s in the desert, the arctic, at sea, in the jungle or from "Monsters", vilagers need to be prepared for any situation. And it’s the Survival, Evasion, Resistance and Escape (SERE) creatures who train them. In this course I Francis N.Stien will instruct you on how to survive in the most hideus and hostile Creatures on the planet. And it’s up to me to make sure that when a mob of angry villagers descends upon a creature doesn’t go as planned, the Vilagers involved are ready for anything. And we mean anything..

Evasion
Why Evade?

When evading villagers Franc employ several effective techniques that I will teach the villagers for both survival and tracking purposes. The tenants of these techiniques are as follows:

  • Assess immediate situation.
  • Assess medical condition; treat as necessary.
  • Gather equipment; determine the need to travel.
  • Sanitize area; hide equipment you decide to leave.
  • Apply initial personal camouflage
  • Do not leave evidence of travel.
  • Travel slowly and deliberately. Intermittently stop, look, listen, and smell.

Escape
Escape Logic

Once Franc has you it is likely that you will be imediatly and brutally "eliminated". Other Creatures may think it interesting to hold you captive. In that ridiculously unlikely sceniario you the villager should conisder the following logic

  • What’s the first thing a villager should do when attempting to escape?
  • When’s the best time to attempt to get away?
  • Should you trust what the hostage taker is telling you?
  • What if you have an opportunity to escape, but your fellow captives might be harmed if you do?
  • Should you comply with demands or fight back?
  • Should you try to come up with a plan if you’re in a group situation?
